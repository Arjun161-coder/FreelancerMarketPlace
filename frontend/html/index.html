Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Freelancers Marketplace? +

Freelancers Marketplace connects clients and freelancers for various projects like development, design, writing, and more.

2. How do I sign up as a freelancer? +

Click "Signup" in the navbar, fill in your details, and choose "Freelancer" as your role.

3. How can I post a project? +

Click "Post Work", then fill in the project form with title, description, skills, and budget.

4. Is there a fee to join? +

No, both clients and freelancers can sign up and use the platform for free.

5. How are freelancers hired? +

Clients browse by skill, view profiles, and assign freelancers based on proposals.

6. Can I upload my resume and portfolio? +

Yes, freelancers can upload resumes and add GitHub or LinkedIn links in their profiles.

7. How will I get notified of updates? +

You’ll get dashboard notifications when clients assign or respond to your application.
